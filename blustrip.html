BlueStrip Application Controlled Blutooth Powerstrip

Project Description

Me and a team of two other students, an electrical engineering major and financing major created a product to compete in Fairfield University Business Plan Competition. We created both a functional prototype product in addition to a formal business plan. We won the first "start up round" of the competition and were granted money to put into building the prototype. Our project consisted of a blutooth enabled powerstrip that through an iPhone application could control power to each outlet individually in order to provide an affordable alternative to more expensive home automation devices.
